Tower of God: What to Know Before the Webtoon's Return

BY XIANWEI WU
News Source

Webtoon's Tower of God has finally returned from hiatus. Before the new chapters drop in English, here's everything you need to know to jump right in.The last update from Webtoon sensation, Tower of God, was Chapter #485 (or Season 3, Episode 68), on June 28, 2020. Since then, due to creator SIU’s health issues, the manhwa has been on the longest hiatus it's ever had, so fans have been clamoring for an update.

WARNING: The following contains major spoilers for Tower of God by SIU, available now in English on Webtoon.

Thankfully, the series finally returned on May 16 with five new chapters on the Korean manhwa platform Naver, meaning it’s only a matter of time before the English version hits Webtoon. Here's everything you need to get up to speed before the series officially returns in English.
